Our Story

Jay and Ynn were introduced by a common friend (Bryan) in high school. Bryan thought of inviting Jay to a Halloween party at Ynn's school and asked her to accompany him for the invitation. Jay agreed, and they became friends—textmates. It seemed as if destiny was making a way to bring their paths together.

However, their story wasn't like the movies where the boy meets the girl and they fall in love immediately. They had a mutual understanding, but it ended due to different priorities in life.

It wasn't until they were in college that they reestablished communication. Throughout high school, Ynn made sure they wouldn't cross paths, but destiny had a different plan. Their gazes met as she rode a pedicab and he walked. Everything seemed to move slowly, like a scene from a movie. They realized that something had not changed. He looked for her on Facebook, asked for her number, and they began talking—conversations that turned into hours, days, weeks, and months. The feeling had returned, but at this point, distance was their adversary. He would be moving to Iloilo to study, and she would be studying in Manila.

It wasn't always rainbows and butterflies, but a long-distance relationship worked for them for four years. After Jay's graduation, they broke up, thinking it was the end of an era. However, a year later, they reconciled and faced life's challenges together.

Jay and Ynn have been together for more than ten years, through thick and thin, both literally and figuratively. They were often asked when they intended to get married, but they planned on waiting until they were emotionally and financially prepared to start a family. It took them some time to get there.

The pair became engaged in 2022, but they also experienced the worst tragedy when Ynn's brother and foster mom passed suddenly, only a month apart. In respect of Kuya Vengie's and Nanay Mila's wishes, they chose to proceed with the wedding this year.

As they say, "the couples that are meant to be are the ones who go through everything that's designed to tear them apart and come out even stronger." The couple is extremely appreciative of their families' love, support, and blessings. This union is evidence that love always triumphs over all obstacles with God's love and mercy.
